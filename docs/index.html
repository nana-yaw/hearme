HearMe

Private dictation for macOS that finds the model that hears your voice best — and never sends a byte anywhere.

Why it exists

Mainstream speech models are benchmarked on American and European voices. On West-African-accented English, the model that tops the leaderboards silently dropped 60–80% of the words in our tests — while another transcribed near-verbatim. You can't know which model fits your voice from a chart. So HearMe measures it: read three short phrases once, and every model on your machine is scored on accuracy and speed against your voice, your microphone, your accent. One click applies the winner.

What it does

Dictate anywhere

Hold a hotkey, speak, release — text appears at your cursor in any app. Live typing (experimental) shows words while you speak.

Voice setup wizard

Benchmarks every downloaded model on your own voice — word-error-rate and speed — and recommends the best fit.

100% local

Whisper & friends run on-device (Metal). No account, no cloud, no telemetry. Works offline forever.

Command mode

Highlight text, hold the shortcut, say "make this more formal" — a local LLM (Ollama) rewrites it in place.

Custom words

Names and jargon spelled right — with one-click suggestions mined from your own dictation history.

Spoken punctuation

"Period", "comma", "new line" become symbols instantly — deterministic, no LLM latency.

Don't trust — verify

lsof -i -a -p $(pgrep -x handy)

Run this while dictating: zero open network connections. The only network use is a one-time, checksum-pinned model download you trigger yourself. Cloud providers aren't disabled — they're removed from the code.
